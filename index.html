Behind ChessEvents

The story behind the platform architectural layout.

As an active chess player who crossed the 1500 ELO milestone on Chess.com, I reached a point where online rapid arenas weren't enough. I wanted to take my game to the physical board and compete in over-the-board tournaments across India.

🔍

That's when I ran into a massive roadblock: There was no reliable, centralized, or user-friendly website to easily track down open chess tournaments in India. Information was scattered across dated notices, messy links, and fragmented PDF circulars.

Frustrated by the friction it took just to find out where and when to play, I decided to engineer a clean solution myself. I developed ChessEvents—a zero-friction data deck designed specifically for Indian players to seamlessly discover, filter, and register for tournaments instantly.
