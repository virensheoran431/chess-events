How to Use ChessEvents

🔍

1. Browse Tournaments

Visit the Tournaments tab to explore all upcoming chess events.

🎯

2. Filter & Search

Use city, Elo rating, format, and sorting options to find the right tournament for you.

❤️

3. Save / Unsave Events

Click the heart button ❤️ / ♡ to save a tournament. Click again to unsave. View them in the Saved tab.

📝

4. Register Easily

Click "Register Now" to open the official registration link.

📢

5. List Your Own Event

Go to List Event and publish your tournament instantly.
